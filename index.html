Welcome to AISC 2024

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence & Sustainable Computing (AISC 2024) provides an environment where ideas are created, exchanged, and synthesized as researchers, practitioners, and educators present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, experiences, and challenges in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Sustainable Computing. The Conference will be held on July 11-13, 2024. AISC 2024 is being organized jointly by Department of Information Technology, B. P. Poddar Institute of Management & Technology, Kolkata & A. K. Choudhury School of Information Technology, University of Calcutta, India. The conference will include presentations of contributed papers, posters along with keynote lectures, and invited talks.

    • Call for Papers

    We invite high quality submissions through research, practice & experience, education, innovative and emerging ideas, describing original and unpublished approaches/results in all areas of artificial intelligence and Sustainable Computing. Submissions will be evaluated on the basis of originality, contribution, soundness, evaluation, quality of presentation and appropriate comparison to related work.

    The topics for AISC 2024 include, but are not limited to-

  • Machine Learning and Sustainable Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Physical System
  • Business Analytics
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Signal Processing System & Artificial Intelligence
  • Artificial Intelligence for Cyber Security
  • Emerging Technology: Blockchain, Quantum Computing, Cognitive Computing, Robotics & Automation
  • Artificial Intelligence for Health Informatics

    Paper Submission

    Submitted papers must comply with Springer plagiarism policy and procedures and must conform to the Springer format of Conference proceedings. Please prepare your paper for a maximum of 10 pages as per the Springer Template. Papers with more than 20% Plagiarism (checked through Turnitin/iThenticate) will not be considered for review.

    It is planned to publish the peer reviewed and selected papers of the conference as proceedings with Springer in their prestigious “Springer Proceedings in Information and Communication Technologies” series (https://www.springer.com/series/17395). For detailed instructions for author and editors of conference proceedings, kindly visit the following link: https://www.springer.com/us/authors-editors/conference-proceedings. Select papers from the conference will be published by Springer as a proceedings book volume. Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers and only papers that pass these checks will be published. Springer Nature does not charge any money for publication of Non-Open Access content. Abstracts/extended abstracts and short papers (less than 4 pages) are not considered for publication.’

  • Please upload the paper using the following link: - https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/AISC2024 on or before the last date for submission.
